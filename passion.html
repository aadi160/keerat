Model United Nations (MUN)

Discussions on International issues with my Father from a very young age sparked by an ability to debate and research. I was introduced to the perfect symbiosis of the Two in 9th grade when my school gave me the opportunity to participate in the intraschool MUN. This resulted in me winning the Best delegate award and me realizing that doing MUNs I something that I enjoy and am naturally good at.MUNs have taught me how to think quick on my feet, strategize and above all effectively orate. I have won awards in various national MUNs such as La Martinere Calcutta and Welham Boys School Dehradun. Through my experience in Muns, I have also received the opportunity to chair MUNs such as the Shri MUN.

 

Football

On one summer morning in 2009, my friend Armaan invited me to play a game of football with friends at his house. Little did I know then, this game alien to me Ould become an integral part of my life. These outings developed into weekly matches amongst friends and soon training, which resulted in tournaments, team selections on a national and international level. I trained under Manoj Singh Bisht, former Haryana C team coach, and ex-professional footballer in India and Anil Katoch, former coach at Arsenal Soccer Schools. I have also had the opportunity to represent BIFA in tournaments in Bangkok and Kuala Lumpur amongst many other competitions.
