I started visiting the centre in the 10th grade. My brother and I visited “The Children of the world “ an Orphanage every week and took basic food supplies for these little children. We slowly started building a bond with them and it took us few months to win their trust and friendship. We tried to teach them basic numbers and simple spoken English and the alphabet. Now, most of the children can speak and read in English, solve simple Math sums and all of them know how to be creative with their activities in their spare time. These activities include drawing, Just a minute speaking, basic Scrabble and Chess. All of these techniques were taught by my brother and I did this over a span 3 year of time and is one something that we do week in and week out even now.
