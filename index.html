ABOUT ME

I’m Keerat, a high school student from New Delhi with a thirst to know more about all things Economics. Most of my time is spent diving head first into research opportunities or in Extra Curricular work on a Football Pitch or a Model United Nations Conference. When I’m not doing the above, you can find me in Uttarakhand servicing the E-Toilets I established through my initiative Keep it Clean Kids or simply Trekking and Travelling. I may also be teaching recreational activities at the Children of The World Orphanage, or educating support staff in my school and condominium on the importance of investing and saving. As you surf through this website, you’ll gain insight as to how I like to keep myself busy and an idea of the research work I have done.
